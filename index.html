About Me

Optimistic, outgoing, and most say likable, I grew up in Greensboro, NC, currently, living in South Florida. I am passionate about networking, sharing ideas, and never getting too comfortable in my state of being. I am consistently living to learn from others, especially those quite different than myself, seeing the invaluable opportunity when receiving others with an open and friendly attitude. I find clarity running through a green-tinted forest trail, probably listening to club music to concentrate on pushing my legs to keep up with my pace, rather than listening to the serenity of the peaceful environment surrounding my sweat filled runs. I funnel energy from those around me and value genuine family and friend relationships above no other. Find me in these situations or keep on exploring!
